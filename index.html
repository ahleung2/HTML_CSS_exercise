List of important header tags that you should add to pages for SEO

Definition: SEO or search engine optimization is the process of improving your website so that it achieves a higher ranking in search engine results pages.

  1. h1: denote the most important text
  2. h2 and h3: often used as subheadings
  3. h4, h5, h6: provide further structure in subsections
  4. Include keywords in header tags!
  5. Only use h1!
